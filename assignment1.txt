Eating Schedule 

Description: This dataset contains information on what food I ate over the span of two weeks. It covers what food I ate, drank, and when and where I consumed this food.

Usage: eating

Format: 
A data frame with 51 rows and 8 columns
8 variables 

Id 
	row number 

date_factor 
	date it occurred on 

Observation 
	number of observations per day

food_type
	name of food item

Drink
	name of drink item

Time
	time of the day the food was eaten

Location
	location of where the food was eaten

Water
	checks to see if the drink is water
